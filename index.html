school achievements

NASA Trip
EXPLORING NASA: A REALTIME SPACE EXPERIENCE ON EARTH

An educational trip to National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) as a part of 10 day trip to USA was organized by the management of K.R. Mangalam World School. It provided its students a golden opportunity to visit Kennedy Space Centre along with other prominent institutions and centers in United States of America in the month of June, 2019.

Interschool Football Tournament
Came first in interschool football tournament

K.R. Mangalam World School Faridabad celebrated its second Inter School Soccer Tournament on April 23-24, 2019 with great zeal and fervour. The scorching heat of summer failed to dent the enthusiasm of the soccer players from 08 schools on the first day, who were eager to take the field.
